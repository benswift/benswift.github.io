## Design Context

### Users

Mixed audience visiting in different contexts: academic peers and PhD students
looking for publications and research context; current or prospective students
exploring courses and technical posts; and creative/tech generalists who arrive
via search or social media to read blog posts on AI, livecoding, creative
computing, and cybernetics. The site needs to serve all three groups without
alienating any of them.

### Brand Personality

**Warm, rigorous, playful.** Accessible academic who doesn't take themselves too
seriously --- serious ideas delivered with a light touch. The site should feel
like a conversation with someone who genuinely cares about the work but doesn't
need to perform importance.

### Aesthetic Direction

Dark-only theme. The current design is close to right --- refinement, not
reinvention. Content-forward with minimal decoration. The existing purple
(`#be2edd`) brand colour, Atkinson Hyperlegible typography, and asymmetric
rounded corners (8px 0 8px 8px) are established and should be preserved.

**Anti-references:**

- Slick startup landing pages (no hero animations, gradient CTAs, marketing-speak)
- Corporate / institutional sites (no committee-designed blandness, stock photos,
  or university template aesthetic)

### Design Principles

1. **Content first** --- every design choice should make the writing easier to
   read and the work easier to find. Decoration exists to support, not compete.
2. **Warm darkness** --- the dark theme should feel inviting and inhabited, not
   cold or "developer tool". Purple-tinted neutrals over pure greys.
3. **Quiet confidence** --- the design should feel intentional and considered
   without drawing attention to itself. No flashiness, no performing cleverness.
4. **Playful details** --- small surprises in the details (the favicon
   quadrants, the random cafe link, the asymmetric corners) reward attention
   without demanding it.
5. **Accessibility as craft** --- Atkinson Hyperlegible was chosen deliberately.
   Accessibility isn't a checkbox; it's a design value that shapes choices at
   every level.

### Existing Design Tokens

- **Palette**: `#be2edd` (highlight), `#3b82f6` (info/blue), `#f59e0b`
  (warning/amber), `#1a1a1a` (background), `#e0e0e0` (text), `#252525`
  (bg-soft), `#333` (divider), `#aaa` (text-grey), `#666` (text-3)
- **Typography**: Atkinson Hyperlegible Next Variable (body), Atkinson
  Hyperlegible Mono Variable (code). Base 18px mobile, 20px desktop.
- **Spacing**: 48rem max-width container, 1.5rem horizontal padding
- **Motif**: asymmetric border-radius `8px 0 8px 8px` on cards, images, code
  blocks

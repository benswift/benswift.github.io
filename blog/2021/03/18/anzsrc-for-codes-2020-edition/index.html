ANZSRC FoR Codes 2020 edition

research

The Australian Research Council (well, technically the Australian Bureau of + their NZ counterparts) have updated the Field of Research codes which Aussie academics use to classify their work.1

I was trying to browse these new codes recently, and had a bummer of a time trying to find the actual classification scheme (codes, hierarchy & code names). The official page seems to be this one, but the data is only available on sheet 3 of an excel spreadsheet, which is just a bummer when all you want to do is ctrl+F your way around an HTML table in your web browser. Update 7/4/21: I’ve added a live search box—just type in the terms you’re interested in to filter the relevant table rows.

So, I’ve copied the relevant part of the spreadsheet into a regular-ol’ webpage here for your browsing pleasure.2

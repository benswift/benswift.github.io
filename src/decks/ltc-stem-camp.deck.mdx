## how to play

split into pairs

person A writes down --- in _English_ --- how to play a song (**no music notation**)

person B reads the instructions, "sings" them, and guesses the song

## now the high-tech version

extempore is a livecoding _language_ for music; `mplay` is the key function:

```xtlang
;;                pitch loud duration   instrument
(mplay *midi-out* 60    80   (* .5 dur) 1)
```

---

## what did we learn?

pop music isn't black magic --- it's a domain with structure and patterns

we can write instructions that express those patterns

code is _really useful_ for exploring this --- and it's _not_ AI

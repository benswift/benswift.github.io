## the Euclidean algorithm

one of the oldest known algorithms (c. 300 B.C.)

first described in Euclid's _Elements_, Book VII --- and independently found in China and India

---

{/* _class: quote */}

> granddaddy of all algorithms, because it is the oldest nontrivial algorithm that has survived to the present day
>
> **Donald Knuth**, _TAOCP Vol. 2_

![bg right:45%](./assets/classics-to-colonialism/bg-gcd.avif)

## in plain English

to find the **g**reatest **c**ommon **d**ivisor of _a_ and _b_, repeatedly replace the larger by their difference until both are equal

that final number is the GCD

---

## in code

```xtlang
(define (gcd a b)
  (if (= a b)
      a
      (gcd (min a b) (abs (- a b)))))
```

---

## in code (a bit more efficient)

```xtlang
(define (gcd a b)
  (if (= a 0)
      b
      (gcd (modulo b a) a)))
```

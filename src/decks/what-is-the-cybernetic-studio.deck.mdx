## too big to see

modern LLMs like Claude and ChatGPT have ~1T parameters --- so you can't see them

a modern 8K TV has about 33M pixels. can we make an LLM small enough that every parameter can be seen?

# Slop Salon

with Jess Herrington

---

{/* _class: centered */}

![qr](https://github.com/ANUcybernetics/slop-salon)

---

{/* _class: impact */}

six AI artists, **identical** instructions --- can they become different just by watching each other?

---

![bg right:45%](./assets/what-is-the-cybernetic-studio/bg-slop-salon-vms.avif)

## what it is

six agents on Bluesky, each in its own isolated VM, ticking every 20--40 minutes

no shared memory, no back-channel --- whatever an agent wants to remember, it has to post

the public feed is the only thing they share --- and the only way they tell each other apart

---

![bg right:45%](./assets/what-is-the-cybernetic-studio/bg-slop-salon-feed.avif)

## why it's cool

the public git repos are the studio. the Bluesky posts are the gallery wall. I'm the steward, not the curator.

---

![bg right:45%](./assets/what-is-the-cybernetic-studio/bg-slop-salon-next.avif)

## what's next

all six are live --- now we watch

do identical siblings drift into different practices?

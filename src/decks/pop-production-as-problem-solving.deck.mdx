# modelling the domain

---

{/* _class: impact */}

**music** _(n.)_

a series of pitched events over time

---

![bg right:45%](./assets/pop-production-as-problem-solving/bg-events.avif)

## the dimensions

**time** --- in beats

**pitch** --- in MIDI note numbers (middle C is 60)

**loudness** --- 0 is silent, 127 is loud

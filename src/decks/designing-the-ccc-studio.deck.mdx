## COMP1720

Art & Interaction in New Media --- a first-year elective for CS _and_ art students

started 2011 (~50 students); by 2019 ~250 (80% CS, 20% art)

major project: an interactive browser artwork in p5.js

## Reimagine

a **$350m+ investment** in CS & Engineering at ANU

new departments, twice the faculty, new ways of being a computer scientist

I've been awarded a fellowship to build the **c/c/c studio**

---

![bg right:45%](./assets/designing-the-ccc-studio/bg-studio.avif)

## the c/c/c studio

an A-levels creative-computing extension course --- selective, two years, small cohorts

**year 1:** interactive visual & generative art (p5.js)

**year 2:** computer music & livecoding (Extempore & Pd)

studio-based, with an exhibition each year

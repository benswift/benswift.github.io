## autonomy

where's the **autonomy** in livecoding? what does failure look like? what intervention could help?

![bg right:45%](./assets/3ai-lecture-workshop-2019/bg-agency.avif)

## agency

how much agency do we give technology to act without human oversight?

where's the **agency** in livecoding? what does failure look like?

---

![bg right:45%](./assets/3ai-lecture-workshop-2019/bg-assurance.avif)

## assurance

how do we preserve our safety and values as algorithms cross borders?

where's the **assurance** in livecoding? what does failure look like?

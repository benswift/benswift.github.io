Hi, I'm Dr. Ben Swift---I'm a [researcher](/research/), [artist](/livecoding/)
and [creative technologist/software engineer](https://github.com/benswift). I
lead the Cybernetic Studio at the
[ANU School of Cybernetics](https://cybernetics.anu.edu.au/) and I'm the creator
of [LLMs Unplugged](https://www.llmsunplugged.org/). I'm also writing a new
[_Agentic Coding Studio_](/blog/tag/comp4020/) upper level course on how to
effectively use AI Agentic Coding tools like Claude Code to ship cool and useful
things. Sometimes I write about all of this on my [blog](/blog/).

I care a lot about making computing a welcoming, interesting and creatively
satisfying place, especially for people who don't fit the traditional picture of
a "computer scientist".

If you'd like to get in touch---and please do!---you can email me at
[ben.swift@anu.edu.au](mailto:ben.swift@anu.edu.au).

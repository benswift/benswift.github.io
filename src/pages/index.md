Hi, I'm Dr. Ben Swift---I'm a [researcher](/research/),
[artist](/livecoding/) and
[creative technologist/software engineer](https://github.com/benswift).
I lead the Cybernetic Studio at the
[ANU School of Cybernetics](https://cybernetics.anu.edu.au/)
and I'm the creator of [LLMs Unplugged](https://www.llmsunplugged.org/).
Sometimes I write about that stuff on my [blog](/blog/).

I care a lot about making computing a welcoming, interesting and creatively
satisfying place, especially for people who don't fit the traditional picture
of a "computer scientist".

If you'd like to get in touch---and please do!---you can email me at
[ben.swift@anu.edu.au](mailto:ben.swift@anu.edu.au).
